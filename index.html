FAQ

KIT QUESTIONS ANSWERED.

Will the leggings survive a sled push? +
Yes. The EffortlessLift™ uses flat-locked seams specifically to prevent chafe under harness straps. Jade Skillen has tested every Hyrox station across multiple World Championship events in this exact kit.
What size should I order for Hyrox? +
Our size guide is based on hip measurement. If between sizes, size up for burpee broad jumps and deep sled pulls — you want full range of motion. Ambassadors compete in true-to-size.
How do I get the 12-week training plan? +
Enter your email above and we'll send it to your inbox immediately. You can also preview the full plan on this page below the kit section. It's completely free — no catch.
Is the plan suitable for beginners? +
The plan is written for three goals: first-timers (just finish), competitive amateurs (sub-60 minutes) and elite development (sub-55 / Elite 15). Select your goal when you sign up and scale the weights accordingly.
What is the returns policy? +
30-day returns on unworn items. If the fit isn't right before race day, contact our team and we'll prioritise an exchange.
